About Us

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1968

Founded by Late Sri Bantwal Raghurama Mukunda Prabhu, SVS College was the first centre of higher education in Bantwal Taluk. Nestled among serene hillocks at Vidyagiri, we have been shaping generations of graduates into independent thinkers, confident individuals, and compassionate leaders.
