How to Apply

Simple and straightforward admission process

Your path to SVS College starts here

Admissions to all UG programmes are based on merit and governed by the Karnataka Government's reservation policy. The process is simple — fill our enquiry form and our team will guide you through every step.

1

Fill Enquiry Form

Submit the online enquiry form on this page. Our team will contact you within 1–2 working days.

2

Visit the College

Come to the campus, collect the application form, and meet with our admissions counsellors.

3

Submit Documents

Submit the filled form along with all required original and photocopied documents.

4

Confirmation

Upon verification, receive your admission confirmation and fee receipt from the college office.
